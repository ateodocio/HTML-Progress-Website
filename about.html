A little
about me !

I am on a self-taught journey, working full time and studying programming in between!

Python was my first language and I'm currently pursuing a Full-Stack roadmap. I started learning on December 2021 and have been documenting my progress on Instagram near the beginning of 2022.

Prior to starting The Odin Project, I pursued a few courses on edx.org that taught me the foundations and basics of Python, along with data structures, algorithms and object-oriented programming.

In the month of April 2022, I took part in the LighthouseLabs 21-Day Data Challenge that taught me some of the basics of matlab, numpy, and SQL.
